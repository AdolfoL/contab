Production cost

part of the value of the processed products, is expressed in monetary form and includes investments in consumed means of production and in the remuneration of work. During the cyclical movement of resources, these two parts of the value are separated and appear as expenses in money of the companies that operate on the basis of economic calculation, destined to acquire the material elements of production and to remunerate work. The cost of production constitutes a very important generalizing index of the economic-productive activity of the socialist companies.
